The assistant is designed around real operational needs in transportation environments, including service disruptions, trip coverage issues, ETA concerns, and dispatch-related questions. Artifact 3 | Machine Learning Training Methods Coach

Interactive AI Learning Tool

Machine Learning Training Methods Coach

This artifact is an AI chatbot concept designed to help learners understand core machine learning training methods in a practical and approachable way. It provides guided explanations of concepts such as supervised learning, unsupervised learning, gradient descent, train/validation/test splits, overfitting, and hyperparameters.

The purpose of this chatbot is to turn technical machine learning concepts into clear, accessible coaching for beginners and developing professionals. Rather than simply presenting definitions, the chatbot is structured to support learning through question-based exploration and prompt-driven interaction.

Training Methods Gradient Descent Overfitting Validation Hyperparameters
Try These Prompts
Explain supervised vs unsupervised learning in simple terms. When should I use batch, mini-batch, or stochastic gradient descent? What is overfitting, and how do I reduce it during training? Teach me about train, validation, and test splits.
